About the Tank Museum

Welcome to the awe-inspiring world of armored warfare, where history comes alive amidst the formidable machines that shaped our past and present. Step into the Tank Museum, a bastion of military heritage and innovation. Nestled within its halls, you'll find an extraordinary collection of tanks and armored vehicles from across the globe, spanning decades of conflict and technological advancement. As you wander through the exhibits, prepare to be transported through time, from the thunderous battles of World War I to the cutting-edge designs of modern warfare. Each tank tells a story of courage, ingenuity, and the ever-evolving nature of combat. Whether you're a seasoned enthusiast, a curious historian, or simply drawn to the power and majesty of these mechanical behemoths, the Tank Museum promises an unforgettable journey into the heart of armored history. So, join us as we explore the triumphs and trials of the armored forces that have shaped the course of nations and the fate of humanity.

The Museum features iconic tanks you may recognise from popular war films such as the M4A3 tank "Fury" the fiercesome "Tiger 131", and the reliant "T34". You may also be intrested in attending our TANKFEST event to see all of the above tanks in action in real life this summer. Dont miss out!
